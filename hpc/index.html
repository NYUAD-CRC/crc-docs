High Performance Computing¶

High Performance Computing Abu Dhabi New York University Abu Dhabi (NYUAD) High Performance Computing (HPC) Center serves New York University Abu Dhabi (NYUAD) researchers, faculty and students by providing them with HPC services and support to help them conduct world-class computational research and education. The HPC Center provides its services through a medium-sized Linux cluster called Dalma. The cluster consists of around 12,000 computing cores and a peak performance of approx. 385 TFLOPS.
