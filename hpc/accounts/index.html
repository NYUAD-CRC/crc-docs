Accounts¶

Important

If you are an external collaborator (not directly employed by NYU), fill this form HPC Collaboration Template.docx with your NYU sponsor, sign and send to us (dalma.admins@nyu.edu).

Requesting New Account¶

  1. Follow the instructions this page to request an new account on Dalma.

  2. Ask your sponsor to approve the request. Follow the instructions in this page to approve account request.

  3. You cannot submit jobs until you pass our online training. Click this: Dalma Trainig

Renewing an Account¶

Users need to renew their account every year.

  1. Follow the instructions in this page to request and renewal of your account on Dalma.

  2. Ask your sponsor to approve the request. Follow the instructions in this page to approve account request

  3. You cannot submit jobs until you pass our online training. Click this: Dalma Training
