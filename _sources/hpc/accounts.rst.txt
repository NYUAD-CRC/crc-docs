.. important::
    If you are an external **collaborator** (not directly employed by NYU), fill this `FORM <https://wikis.nyu.edu/download/attachments/82052131/HPC%20Collaboration%20Template.docx?version=1&modificationDate=1507467651400&api=v2>`_ with your NYU sponsor, sign and send to us (dalma.admins@nyu.edu).


Requesting New Account
----------------------

#. Follow the instructions this `page`_ to request an new account on Dalma.
#. Ask your sponsor to approve the request. Follow the instructions in this `page`_ to approve account request.
#. You cannot submit jobs until you pass our online training. Click this: `Dalma Training`_


Renewing an Account
-------------------
Users need to renew their account every year.

#. Follow the instructions in this page to request and renewal of your account on Dalma. 
#. Ask your sponsor to approve the request. Follow the instructions in this page to approve account request
#. You cannot submit jobs until you pass our online training. Click this: Dalma Training
